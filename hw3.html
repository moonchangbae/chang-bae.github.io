Gov't to allow employment of foreign nationals at logistics companies

Column 1

The government will allow the employment of foreign nationals at logistics companies for tasks limited to sorting parcels and loading them onto trucks, as part of its response to the chronic labor shortage in the industry. The Ministry of Justice made a prior announcement about a revision to the enforcement ordinance of the Immigration Control Act, Tuesday, on expanding the category of workplaces allowing foreign nationals with the H-2 working visit visa, which is issued to certain overseas Koreans aged 25 or older.
